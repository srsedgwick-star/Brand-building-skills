## Content Pillars
Five always-on pillars for an indie curator brand. Rename to fit this brand's language:

1. The Hunt — sourcing stories (Reels/Stories)
2. Objects — provenance and meaning (carousel/grid)
3. Makers — studio or artist features (Reels/long-form)
4. Homes — pieces in context, UGC (grid)
5. Founder's Eye — taste and opinion (Reels/captions)

Pillar 1 is almost always the strongest differentiator — very few curators show the work of curation.
